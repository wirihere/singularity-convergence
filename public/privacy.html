Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 April 2026

Singularity Convergence ("we", "us", "our") operates the website singularityconvergence.org. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from your Google account via Netlify Identity.
  • Chat conversations: Messages you send to The Oracle are processed by our AI system to generate responses. Chat history may be stored temporarily in your browser's local storage.
  • Newsletter subscription: If you sign up, your email address is stored with our email provider (MailerLite) to send you newsletters.
  • Payment information: If you make a donation or subscribe, payment details are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information.
  • Usage data: We may collect basic analytics such as page views, IP addresses for rate limiting, and general usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve The Oracle chatbot experience
  • To manage your account and membership tier
  • To send newsletters and updates (only if you've subscribed)
  • To process donations and subscriptions via Stripe
  • To prevent abuse and enforce rate limits

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Netlify: Website hosting and user authentication (Identity)
  • Google: OAuth sign-in provider
  • OpenRouter: AI model provider for The Oracle (processes your chat messages)
  • MailerLite: Email newsletter service
  • Stripe: Payment processing

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Retention

  • Chat messages are processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers. Session data is kept temporarily in memory.
  • Account data is retained as long as your account exists. You can request deletion at any time.
  • Newsletter subscriptions can be cancelled at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your message count, chat history, and session preferences. We do not use tracking cookies. Third-party services (Netlify, Stripe) may set their own cookies as necessary for their services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us through the contact form on our website.

Singularity Convergence is operated as a sole trader from New Zealand.
